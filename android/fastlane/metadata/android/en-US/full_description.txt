CompositesAI is an AI-powered platform designed specifically for the composites engineering industry. Ask questions about material design, calculations, and process optimization in natural language — and get instant, expert-level answers.

AI Chat Assistant:
• Ask anything about composites engineering and get domain-specific answers instantly
• Voice input — dictate your questions hands-free
• Attach images and files to your conversations for deeper analysis
• Use integrated tools for more accurate, context-aware answers
• Share, pin, and manage your chat history

Composite Material Calculators:
1. Calculate effective beam stiffness matrix for beam model
2. Calculate A, B, D matrices, transverse shear stiffness matrix, in-plane properties, and flexural properties for the plate model
3. Calculate effective solid stiffness matrix and engineering constants for the solid model
4. Calculate the coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE) for thermoelastic analysis
5. Use Gmsh as a mesh generator to generate mesh and images

Supported Composite Models:
• Laminate
• Unidirectional fibre-reinforced composite
• Honeycomb sandwich structure
• Particle reinforced composite
